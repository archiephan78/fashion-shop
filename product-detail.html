Maintenance

  1. When you bought home your beloved wedding dress, after the wedding, you need to preserve it careful and forever. But if you just leave it to a small laundry on the street, you may be regretful, because the maintenance of this wedding dress is different from other expensive clothes. The simple thing you need to do is to know some little tips, and it won't be hard for you to clean and preserve wedding dress by yourself.
  2. Wet cleaning is the key of washing wedding dress, because the little decorations on the wedding dress like beadings or lace can not stand the erosion from chemical materials during dry cleaning. The best way is soak wedding dress in the water with mild neutral detergent for a while but not too long. In this way you can clean the stain like red wine, or lard, and even invisible perspiration or white wine can be cleaned.
  3. After you cleaned wedding dress and dry thoroughly, then you can collect and preserve it in the cool and dry place, such as closet and even under the bed. But remember, please do not hang it up, because even just the weight of wedding dress has vertical force, which may lengthen it and even tear it.
  4. Last but not the least, before you preserve your wedding dress, please do remember clean your hands, and do not stuck on makeup, otherwise, there will be small macula lute on the dress for a long time. What's more, the skirt hoop in the wedding dress can be twisted, but every year, when you ted clothes, please recover the shape of skirt hoop, or it will lose elasticity.
